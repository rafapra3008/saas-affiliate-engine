Webflow is built for **professional agencies, freelance developers, and marketing teams** requiring total creative control and high-performance output.

**Main Use Cases:**
*   Building high-conversion marketing sites and landing pages.
*   Developing custom e-commerce stores with powerful features.
*   Managing scalable content (CMS) projects for clients.

**Key Features:**
*   **Visual CSS Editing:** Access complex CSS properties and Flexbox/Grid layouts through an intuitive visual canvas.
*   **Integrated CMS:** A powerful, flexible Content Management System built directly into the design process.
*   **Advanced Interactions & Lottie:** Create dynamic animations and sophisticated user interactions without custom JavaScript.
*   **High-Performance Hosting:** Secure, reliable hosting (built on AWS and Fastly) that scales automatically.

**Pricing Overview:**
Webflow offers a robust **Free Starter Plan** for prototyping and learning. Pricing scales based on complexity and necessary features.
