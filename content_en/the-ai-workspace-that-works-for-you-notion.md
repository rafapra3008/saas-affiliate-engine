Knowledge workers, scaling startups, remote teams, and content creators who need to consolidate their organizational tools and documentation. If you manage projects, draft content, or maintain a company wiki, Notion is designed to be your centralized "source of truth."

*   **Project Management:** Track complex tasks, timelines, and sprints using customizable Kanban boards, calendars, and list views.

*   **Content Creation:** Utilize Notion AI to instantly summarize meetings, brainstorm outlines, translate text, and generate first drafts for blog posts or emails.
*   **Custom Databases:** Build lightweight CRMs, asset trackers, content calendars, or personal dashboards tailored exactly to your needs.
